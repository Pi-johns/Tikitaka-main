Tikitaka Pool Tables – Kenya’s Trusted Game Room Experts

With over a decade of experience, Tikitaka Pool Tables has become a household name in Kenya's recreational scene. Based in Nairobi, we specialize in crafting high-quality pool tables, table tennis setups, foosball tables, and dartboards. Our commitment to excellence has led to the establishment of branches in Mombasa, Nakuru, Narok, and Eldoret, ensuring that quality gaming experiences are accessible nationwide.

Whether you're looking to upgrade your game room or seeking professional after-sale services, Tikitaka Pool Tables is your go-to destination. Our products are designed with precision, ensuring durability and an unmatched gaming experience.
